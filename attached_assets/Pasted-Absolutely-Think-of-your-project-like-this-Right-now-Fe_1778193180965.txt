```text
record 1001 window 1 → train
record 1001 window 2 → test
```

That is cheating because the model has already seen the same patient/record.

---

## Step 8: Train the model

Train two parts:

### Part A: XGBoost

This is the strong simple model.

It learns from your clinical CTG features.

### Part B: ReserveNet

This is your unique model.

It combines:

```text
CTG features
+
fetal reserve
+
deceleration burden
+
contraction response
+
uncertainty
```

Later, you can add PulseFM, the raw-signal encoder.
